Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2024

1. Data Collection

The DAC-Ruby Calculator is built with privacy-first principles:

  • 🔒 No data collection: We don't request or store personal information
  • 📱 Local processing: All computations happen entirely on your device
  • 🌐 No tracking: Zero analytics or third-party tracking tools

2. Storage

Regarding data storage:

  • 💾 Full control: Results are saved only at your explicit request
  • 📁 Device-only: Files are stored solely in your device's download folder
  • 🔄 No sync: We don't backup to cloud services

3. Permissions

The app requires only:

  • 📁 Storage: Exclusively for manual result exports
  • 🔋 Minimal access: No contacts, location or sensitive data access
